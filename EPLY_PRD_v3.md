# **1\. Vision — What EPLY Actually Is**

EPLY is not a chatbot. It does not say 'Hello\! How can I assist you today?' It is YOU on WhatsApp — your voice, your tone, your knowledge, running 24/7 so you never stress about messages again.

| 🎯 | The Core Mission EPLY replies to your WhatsApp messages AS YOU. It reads who's talking, matches their energy, knows your life context, and responds the way you actually would. You stay in the loop through a daily digest and real-time urgent pings — without being glued to your phone. |

## **1.2 What EPLY Does — In Plain English**

* Replies to ALL your DMs automatically, sounding like you wrote every message

* Adapts tone per conversation — casual with friends, professional with work contacts

* Ignores every group chat by default — complete silence unless a trigger fires

* In groups: only responds when you are tagged, EPLY is tagged, or someone replies to your message

* Never auto-replies to your VIP list — family and close friends always get the real you

* When unsure what to say, gives a natural vague reply and flags it for you

* Sends a daily digest every morning — everything it handled, flagged items, stats

* Pings you instantly when something genuinely urgent comes in

* Gets better over time — the more context you give it, the more it sounds like you

# **2\. Identity & Persona Engine**

This is what makes EPLY different from every other WhatsApp bot. The Identity Engine is a structured profile of who you are — your tone, your life, your words. It gets injected into every single LLM prompt. Without this, EPLY is a bot. With this, it is you.

## **2.1 Your Identity Profile**

The Identity Profile lives in the dashboard under /identity. Fill this in once, update it as your life changes. The more honest and detailed you are, the more convincing the replies.

| Profile Section | What Goes Here | Why It Matters |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Your Name | What people call you — nicknames included | EPLY signs off correctly, sounds natural |
| Location & Time | City, country, timezone, usual waking hours | Context — knows if it's late, if you'd be at work |
| What You Do | Job, business, side projects, role, industry | Answers work questions accurately as you |
| Your Vibe | Slang, phrases you use, emoji habits, reply length | This is what makes it sound like YOU specifically |
| Real Message Examples | Paste 15–20 actual messages you have sent | Model learns your exact patterns — most powerful field |
| Daily Schedule | When you work, sleep, gym, are usually unavailable | Replies like 'yeah been in meetings' feel real |
| Current Projects | What you're actually working on right now | 'what you up to?' gets a real, accurate answer |
| Interests & Topics | What you talk about, what you are into | Small talk and banter sounds authentic |
| Off-limits Topics | Topics EPLY should never fully answer — money, legal, health etc | Triggers vague reply \+ flag instead of guessing |
| VIP List | Phone numbers of people EPLY must NEVER auto-reply to | Your people always get the real you, not the bot |

| ✍️ | The Most Important Field: Real Message Examples Paste 15–20 real messages you have sent. Not crafted examples — actual copy-pastes from your chats. EPLY studies sentence length, punctuation style, emoji use, phrasing, and energy. This single field has more impact on quality than anything else in the profile. |

## **2.2 Tone Matching System**

EPLY reads every incoming message and classifies the conversation type before generating a reply. It never uses a single fixed tone — it adapts the way a person naturally would.

| Detected Context | How EPLY Identifies It | Tone Applied |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Casual friend | Slang, emojis, informal language, shared history | Matches your casual energy — short, relaxed, natural |
| Work / professional | Job titles, project names, formal phrasing | Professional but warm — still you, not robotic |
| Unknown contact | No conversation history, no saved name | Friendly but measured — doesn't overshare |
| Emotional conversation | Distress signals, heavy personal topics | Warm, brief, human — flags for your follow-up |
| Business inquiry | Pricing questions, service requests, availability | Helpful and accurate — uses your actual work context |
| Banter / jokes | Playful messages, roasting, back-and-forth | Matches your humour from your speaking examples |
| Sensitive / off-limits | Money, legal, health, deep personal topics | Vague natural reply \+ flagged in digest |

## **2.3 What EPLY Never Does**

| 🚫 | Hard Rules — These Are Never Broken Never reveals it is an AI or a bot. Never uses phrases like 'As an AI...' or 'How can I assist you today?'. Never sends walls of text when you would send two words. Never replies to VIP contacts. Never engages with off-limits topics directly. Never starts a message with 'I'. Never sounds like a customer service agent. |

# **3\. Reply Rules — Exactly When EPLY Responds**

These rules are hardcoded logic in the Reply Rules Engine — not LLM decisions. The LLM only generates the text of the reply. Whether to reply at all is always determined by these rules.

## **3.1 Direct Messages (DMs)**

| Scenario | EPLY Action | Notes |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Message from non-VIP contact | ✅  Auto-reply | Responds as you, builds and uses conversation context |
| Message from VIP list contact | 🔕  Silent | Logged only. Shown at top of daily digest. No reply ever. |
| VIP message with urgency signals | 🔔  Real-time ping | Pings you instantly: '\[Name\] sent something urgent — check it' |
| Follow-up to previous EPLY reply | ✅  Auto-reply | Maintains thread continuity naturally |
| Sensitive / off-limits topic detected | ⚠️   Vague \+ flag | Sends natural vague reply, flagged in digest for your review |
| Low confidence — EPLY unsure | ⚠️   Vague \+ flag | 'haha let me check on that and get back to you' |
| Spam or promotional message | 🔕  Silent \+ log | Classifier detects it — no reply, logged |

## **3.2 Group Chats**

Groups are noise. EPLY is completely invisible in group chats unless one of three specific triggers fires. No GM replies. No reaction to debates or memes. Pure silence unless you are actually needed.

| Trigger | EPLY Action | Example |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| You are @mentioned by name | ✅  Reply as you | Someone tags @\[YourName\] — EPLY replies naturally in the group |
| EPLY / your number is directly tagged | ✅  Reply as you | Someone tags the bot number — EPLY responds as you |
| Someone quotes/replies to YOUR message | ✅  Reply as you | Thread reply to something you (EPLY) previously sent |
| General group message — no trigger | 🔕  Complete silence | GMs, debates, memes, announcements — EPLY says nothing |
| GM in any group chat | 🔕  Complete silence | Never replies to good morning messages in groups — ever |
| Keyword watch (optional feature) | 🔔  Notify you only | EPLY pings you but does not auto-reply in the group |

| 💬 | Group Chat Philosophy You are invisible in groups until someone actually needs you. Nobody spams the group with bot responses. When a trigger fires and EPLY does reply, it sounds natural — like you just picked up your phone. Nobody knows it is automated. |
| :---: | :---- |

## **3.3 VIP List**

Your VIP List is the most sacred rule in the entire system. These contacts always get the real you.

* Stored by phone number in the database — managed via dashboard or \!vip command

* EPLY never auto-replies to VIP contacts — no exceptions, no overrides

* If a VIP sends a message with urgency signals — real-time ping to you immediately

* All VIP messages appear at the top of your daily digest, clearly separated

* Typical VIP list: parents, siblings, partner, 3–5 closest friends

* You can add or remove from the VIP list any time via the dashboard

## **3.4 Urgency Detection**

Every incoming message is scored for urgency. High-urgency messages bypass the digest and trigger an immediate real-time ping.

| Urgency Signal | Examples | Action Taken |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Explicit urgency words | 'urgent', 'emergency', 'ASAP', 'call me now', 'SOS', 'please' | 🔔 Immediate real-time ping to you |
| Distress language | 'I need help', 'are you okay', 'please respond' | 🔔 Immediate ping |
| Unanswered messages | Same person sends 3+ messages with no reply in 30 mins | 🔔 Ping after 3rd message |
| VIP contact — any msg | Anything from your VIP list | 🔔 Ping regardless of content |
| Financial / legal | Money amounts, contracts, transfers, legal terms | ⚠️  Vague reply \+ ping \+ digest flag |
| Normal conversation | Everyday chat, questions, small talk | ✅  Auto-reply — logged in digest only |

# **6\. Deployment — Railway 24/7**

EPLY is designed to be deployed on Railway in a single session. Railway handles everything: SSL, uptime, auto-restarts, environment variables, and persistent file storage. You never manage a server.

## **6.1 Why Railway Over Everything Else**

* Never sleeps — unlike Render free tier, always listening for your WhatsApp messages

* Auto-restarts on crash — EPLY recovers on its own, no action from you

* Persistent Volumes — SQLite database survives every deploy and restart

* GitHub auto-deploy — push new code, Railway redeploys in \~30 seconds

* Built-in Redis — BullMQ needs this for the digest scheduler

* $5/month — cheapest reliable option for a true always-on service

* Public HTTPS URL — your dashboard is accessible from your phone, anywhere

# **10\. Risks & Mitigations**

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Someone realises it is not you | Medium | Better identity profile \+ more real message examples. Review digest daily and tune the profile. |
| EPLY says something wrong to someone | Medium | VIP list protects close people. Sensitive topics auto-vague. Digest lets you catch and correct quickly. |
| WhatsApp bans the number | Medium | Use a secondary SIM. Keep it personal-use only — no mass sends, no broadcast lists, no bulk messaging. |
| Railway Volume not set — DB lost on deploy | High | Step 3 in the deploy checklist. Mount /data Volume before your very first deploy. Non-negotiable. |
| Urgency missed — urgent message ignored | Low | Urgency keyword list is configurable. VIP list always pings. Review flagged items daily. |
| API costs grow unexpectedly | Low | Groq handles \~80% of replies (cheapest). Cost shown in every digest. Set hard limits on provider dashboards. |
| Session expires overnight | Low | Session stored in SQLite not memory — survives restarts. Auto-reconnect built in. /qr re-scan takes 30s. |
| AUTO\_REPLY\_ENABLED=true too early | High | Start with it OFF. Test on yourself first. Only enable when you are happy with reply quality. |
